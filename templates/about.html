About Us

Our Fake News Detection system is an advanced machine learning-based application designed to identify and classify news articles as real or fake. With the increasing spread of misinformation, our goal is to provide users with a reliable tool to verify news authenticity.

Our Mission

We aim to combat misinformation by leveraging cutting-edge Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and machine learning models to analyze and detect fake news accurately.

How It Works

  • Users input a news headline.
  • Our system processes the text using a pre-trained ML model.
  • The model predicts whether the news is real or fake.
  • The result is displayed instantly.
